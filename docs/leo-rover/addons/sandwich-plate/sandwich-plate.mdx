## Introduction

The elevated mounting plate enhances the functionality of the Leo Rover by
providing an additional mounting surface for LiDARs, cameras, and other
equipment. This accessory includes a mounting plate with 7 mm diameter thru
holes, eight M5 threaded distances of 40 mm, and four M5x10 mounting screws,
allowing for a 40 or 80 mm elevation above the original mounting plate. The
straightforward assembly leverages the existing threaded inserts of the mounting
plate.

### What's included

- 1 x Mounting plate
- 8 x M5 male-female threaded distance, 40 mm in length
- 4 x ISO 7380-2 M5x10 screws

| Main materials           |        Aluminum         |

| Hole grid                |      18 mm x 15 mm      |
| Mounting holes diameter  |          7 mm           |

:::note

Holes in mounting plates are designed for KFS2-M5 press-in nuts
([example](https://catalog.pemnet.com/item/nuts-broaching-types-kf2-kfs2/broaching-nuts-types-kf2-kfs2-metric/kfs2-m5)),
but it is also possible to use standard M5/M6 nuts and screws.

:::

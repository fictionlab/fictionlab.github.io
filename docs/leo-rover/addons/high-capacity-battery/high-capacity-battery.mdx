High capacity battery for the Leo Rover. It's a high-capacity Li-Ion 3S battery,
estimated to last up to 10 hours of driving or 20 hours of video streaming, with
a 3-hour charge time using a 5 A charger.

:::warning

Due to restrictions on battery transportation, we are only able to ship High
capacity batteries within inland Europe.

:::

<Product />

## Main parameters

| Parameter                        | Value                                                              |
| -------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Battery type                     | Li-Ion 3S1P                                                        |
| Battery capacity                 | 15 mAh                                                             |
| Estimated charging time (0-100%) | 3 hours (with 5A charger) \ 8 hours (with standard Leo 2A charger) |
| Main materials                   | Aluminum, PLA                                                      |

## Additional information

The battery is estimated to last for up to 10 hours of nominal driving or up to
20 hours of video streaming.

The battery comes with a 5 A charger with which it can be fully charged (0-100%)
in approximately 3 hours with nominal current. With the standard charger (2 A),
the charging time (0-100%) is about 8 hours.

Introducing our universal camera mast, crafted with a sturdy aluminum tube, PLA
mounting stand, and Manfrotto 234RC head. This versatile solution offers 180°
vertical pan/tilt and horizontal rotation, ensuring secure mounting for most
cameras. Easily attach it to your robot's chassis for high-quality footage.
Cameras not included. Available on demand.

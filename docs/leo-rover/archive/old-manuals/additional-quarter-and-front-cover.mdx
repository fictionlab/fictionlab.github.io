## Step 1: Front cover

Prepare:

- 1x mounting plate (aluminum, painted grey)
- 1x front cover (3D-printed)
- 4x M4x10 (or M4x8) torx screw
- 4x M4 square nut
- T10 torx screwdriver

## Step 2: Additional quarter

Prepare:

- 1x additional quarter metal plate (aluminum, painted grey)
- 1x additional quarter cover (3D-printed)
- 2x M4x8 torx screw
- 2x M4 square nut
- T10 torx screwdriver

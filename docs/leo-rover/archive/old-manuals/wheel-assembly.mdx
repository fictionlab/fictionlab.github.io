## Preparation

### Assembly time

20 min per wheel / 80 min total

### Tools needed

- cross-head screwdriver
- 2.5mm hex key (metric)
- 3mm hex key (metric)
- lithium grease
- Loctite glue (anaerobic, low-strength)
